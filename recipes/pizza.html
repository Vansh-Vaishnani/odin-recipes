Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is a crowd-pleaser, featuring a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey mozzarella cheese.

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza crust on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the crust to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, followed by pepperoni and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Season with oregano and garlic powder.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
